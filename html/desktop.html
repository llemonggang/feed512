STORY

//horizontal line

Feed512 is a non-profit organization that digitally connects local restaurants and markets with food charities to provide a solution for Austin's food waste dilemma. Through this website, we hope to establish a digital partnership that will empower givers and receivers to make a difference in the community.


Feed512 provides a user-friendly interface which includes scheduling and travel preferences, along with a message center to keep track of your community service history. We empower charities to choose the category of food they need along with the category of food that is being offered in order make the perfect match to reduce food waste in our city.
